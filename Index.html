About Flappy Bird Game

Flappy Bird is a simple yet challenging arcade game where you guide a bird through pipes by tapping or clicking to keep it in the air. Every pipe you pass increases your score. This free web version is mobile-friendly, fast, and fun to play anywhere!

How to Play

  • Tap or click to make the bird fly.
  • Avoid hitting the pipes or the ground.
  • Each pipe passed adds one point to your score.
  • Try to beat your high score!
